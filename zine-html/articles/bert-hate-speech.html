Model Choice

Training

Fine-tuning used a learning rate of 2e-5, weighted cross-entropy loss to address class imbalance (explicit hate speech is rare in parliamentary speech by design), and early stopping on validation F1.

Results

After fine-tuning for 5 epochs:
